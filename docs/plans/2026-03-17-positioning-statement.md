# Skygest — Positioning Statement

## Value Proposition

**For** energy analysts, journalists, and domain experts who track how energy markets, policy, and technology are understood and debated

**that need** to find, understand, and ground the high-signal expert discussions that drive energy decision-making — without manually hunting across feeds, newsletters, and data sources

**Skygest**

**is an** expert energy intelligence reader

**that** surfaces expert threads ranked by signal quality, preserves the discourse and data that make them valuable, and connects expert analysis to underlying data sources — building a living record of how energy narratives evolve.

## Differentiation Statement

**Unlike** editorial newsletters and manual feed monitoring

**Skygest**

**provides** direct access to the actual expert discourse — not one editor's summary of it — with charts rendered, sources linked, discussion preserved, and claims grounded through data integrations.

## Notes

- Category "expert energy intelligence reader" is a working label — anchors the media/document reading metaphor
- Curation is human-directed via MCP, not autonomous AI — the user (or their agent) shapes what surfaces
- The expert layer is the core: experts are data refineries, and ingesting them captures both refined outputs and raw inputs
